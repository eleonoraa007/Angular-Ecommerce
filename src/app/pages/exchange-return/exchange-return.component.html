Exchange & Return Policy

Exchange Policy

We want you to love your purchase! If you're not satisfied with your product, you can exchange it within 30 days of delivery. The item must be in its original condition, unused, and with all original tags and packaging.

Steps for Exchange:

  1. Contact our customer support to initiate the exchange process.
  2. Provide the order details and specify the item you want to exchange.
  3. Ship the product back using the provided return label.
  4. Once we receive the product, the new item will be shipped within 5 business days.

Return Policy

If you'd like to return an item, you can do so within 30 days of purchase. The product must be in its original condition, unused, and with all original packaging. Refunds will be processed within 7 business days after we receive the returned item.

Steps for Return:

  1. Contact customer support to initiate the return process.
  2. Provide your order details and the reason for returning.
  3. Use the return label provided to ship the product back to us.
  4. Once the product is received, your refund will be processed within 7 business days.

Note: Shipping fees are non-refundable. For more information, please contact our support team.
